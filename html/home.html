FAQS

What Is JeniusAcademy?

JeniusAcademy is an online learning platform designed to support students from elementary school to those preparing for college entrance exams. Our platform offers interactive learning tools, personalized tutoring, diverse exercises, progress tracking, and a discussion forum to enhance the educational experience.

How can I register for JeniusAcademy

To register, navigate to the Register Page on our website. You will need to provide your first name, surname, email, phone number, password, and password confirmation. Ensure that all fields are filled out correctly and meet the validation requirements.

What subscription packages does JeniusAcademy offer?

We offer a variety of subscription packages tailored to different learning needs. Each package includes a range of benefits, such as access to exclusive content, personalized tutoring sessions, and progress tracking. Visit the Subscription Page for detailed information on each package, including pricing and benefits.

How do I access the learning materials and videos?

Once you have subscribed, you can access the learning materials and videos on the Learn Page. This page provides a comprehensive set of resources, including instructional videos, learning objectives, teacher information, and related exercises. All content is organized to help you achieve the best learning outcomes.

How can I get help if I have questions or issues?

If you have any questions or encounter any issues, you can visit our discussion forum for support from our community and tutors. Additionally, our FAQ section provides answers to common questions. For further assistance, you can contact our support team directly through the Contact Us page.
